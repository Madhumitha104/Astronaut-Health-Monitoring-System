# Literature Review
- Astroskin NASA (2020)
- Wearable Biosensors (2023)
- Edge AI Monitoring (2024)


## 1. Astroskin – NASA (2020)
**Paper:** "Astroskin: Smart Garment for Continuous Health Monitoring"
**Work:** Wearable garment with respiration and activity sensors for real-time monitoring.
**Limitation:** Bulky for long-duration use.

## 2. Wearable Biosensors – Dcosta et al. (2023)
**Paper:** Frontiers in Space Technologies
**Work:** Flexible pulse oximetry, temperature and motion sensors for microgravity.
**Limitation:** Signal artifacts due to microgravity and motion.

## 3. Edge AI Monitoring – Mani et al. (2024)
**Paper:** npj Microgravity
**Work:** On-device AI for ECG arrhythmia detection and anomaly alerts.
**Limitation:** Small dataset; needs real astronaut data validation.

## 4. Telemedicine in Space – Urlings (2024)
**Work:** Onboard preprocessing and delayed telemetry to Earth.
**Limitation:** Bandwidth limitations in deep-space missions.

## 5. Flexible Patch Monitoring – Toscano (2021)
**Work:** Patch-based monitoring with improved comfort and mobility.
**Limitation:** Short battery life; motion artifacts present.
